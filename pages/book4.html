About The Le Royal Hotel:

Le Royal Hotel Amman, one of the best 5 stars hotels in Amman - Jordan, offers breathtaking panoramic views and an award-winning lush Wellness experience. This luxurious hotel offers an award winning, internationally acclaimed standard of service, exemplified eight intercontinental dining experiences, and luxurious event facilities.


Le Royal Hotel Amman stands as one of the premier 5-star hotels in Amman, Jordan, offering guests breathtaking panoramic views and an award-winning wellness experience. This luxurious establishment is renowned for its internationally acclaimed standard of service, which is exemplified by its eight intercontinental dining experiences and opulent event facilities.


Whether you're indulging in the lush wellness amenities or savoring the diverse culinary offerings, Le Royal Hotel Amman provides an unparalleled level of luxury and sophistication. With its exceptional service and prime location, it ensures that every guest enjoys a truly memorable stay.


From the moment you arrive, you'll be captivated by the stunning architecture and elegant design that define Le Royal Hotel Amman. The hotel's spacious and beautifully appointed rooms and suites provide the perfect sanctuary for relaxation and rejuvenation. For those seeking relaxation and pampering, the hotel's lush wellness facilities include a state-of-the-art spa, fitness center, and an array of holistic treatments. Guests can unwind and indulge in a serene atmosphere, ensuring a sense of well-being and tranquility throughout their stay.


Culinary enthusiasts will be delighted by the hotel's diverse dining options, which feature a range of international cuisines prepared by world-class chefs. Whether you're in the mood for Mediterranean delicacies, Asian fusion, or classic European dishes, Le Royal Hotel Amman's dining venues promise a gastronomic journey that will satisfy even the most discerning palate.
