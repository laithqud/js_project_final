About The Mövenpick Hotel:

We dish up delicious pleasures, just to your liking, from surprising new flavours and nourishing bites to a menu of services to sweeten your stay. But serving the good life means nourishing the environment, our people and local communities too, because at Mövenpick, indulgence comes full of care from us, carefree for you.


At Mövenpick, we take pride in offering delightful experiences that cater to your every taste. From tantalizing new flavors and wholesome bites to a comprehensive range of services designed to enhance your stay, we aim to serve the good life in every way.


However, our commitment to indulgence goes beyond just satisfying your cravings. We believe that true luxury involves caring for the environment, our people, and local communities. At Mövenpick, indulgence is not only about personal enjoyment but also about ensuring that our actions are sustainable and responsible. We carefully source ingredients, support local producers, and implement eco-friendly practices to nourish both the planet and our guests.


By prioritizing sustainability and community well-being, we make sure that every moment of your stay is filled with care and thoughtfulness. This way, you can enjoy carefree indulgence, knowing that it comes from a place of genuine concern and respect for the world around us. At Mövenpick, we seamlessly blend pleasure with purpose, creating an experience that is both luxurious and conscientious.
