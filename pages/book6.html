About The Petra Sella Hotel:

Is a newly renovated and well furnished four stars hotel, finishing 5 stars, located in Wadi Mousa City. The hotel is about 3 minutes walk to the city center and 900 meters away or 15 minutes walk to the entrance of the famous Petra Archaeological Park.


Our hotel, located in the heart of Wadi Mousa City, has been newly renovated and exquisitely furnished to provide an exceptional experience for our guests. As a four-star establishment nearing five-star standards, we offer comfort and luxury at every turn.


Conveniently situated just a 3-minute walk from the city center, our hotel is ideally positioned for guests to explore the vibrant local area. Additionally, we are only 900 meters away, or a leisurely 15-minute walk, from the entrance of the renowned Petra Archaeological Park.


Our prime location, combined with our commitment to outstanding service and top-notch amenities, ensures that your stay with us will be both memorable and comfortable. Whether you're here for a relaxing getaway or an adventurous journey through history, our hotel is the perfect starting point for your explorations.
