About The Corwn Plaza Hotel:

The Crowne Plaza Amman hotel is perched on Amman's highest peak, offering easy access to a major shopping district and only four kilometers from the new city center (Abdali).


Guests can unwind at the Black Iris Rooftop with a delightful cocktail, taking in a stunning 270-degree view during sunset. Alternatively, they can enjoy mouthwatering steaks at V-Lounge or have informal coffee meetings at Jasmine Lobby Lounge. The dedicated staff ensures a seamless business experience, backed by a 24-hour Business Center.


Indulge in self-care at our spa or take a relaxing dip in our heated indoor or outdoor pools. Stay active and fit in our well-equipped gym at your own pace.


Our concierge is ready to guide you through exploring our beautiful country, from Petra, Aqaba, and Wadi Rum in the south to the captivating landscapes of the north. Notably, we are the nearest hotel to Queen Alia International Airport and conveniently located within a 15-minute drive of most embassies without traffic congestion.


Our hotel boasts five strategically placed meeting rooms, each capable of hosting conferences and events for up to 1,000 guests. Our event and wedding facilities are uniquely exceptional, ranging from the elegant Nabataean Ballroom to the picturesque outdoor area by the pool.
